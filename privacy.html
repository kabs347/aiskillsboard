Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 19, 2026

AI Skills Board ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

A) Information you provide

  • Name and email address (e.g., to access exams and receive results/certificates)
  • Support messages you send us

B) Exam-related information

  • Exam attempts, responses, scores, and pass/fail outcomes
  • Certification type purchased and completed

C) Payment information

Payments are processed by a third-party payment provider. We do not store full payment card details. We may receive transaction metadata such as purchase confirmation, amount, and product purchased.

D) Website usage data

We may collect basic usage data such as device type, browser type, pages visited, and approximate location. This may be collected via cookies or similar tools.

2) How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver exam access and provide customer support
  • Administer exams, scoring, and retakes
  • Issue certificates and credential IDs (when applicable)
  • Provide credential verification (if you share your credential link)
  • Improve our assessments, website, and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and protect exam integrity
  • Comply with legal obligations

3) Legal basis (where applicable)

We process personal data based on:

  • Performance of a contract (delivering the service you purchased)
  • Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, product improvement)
  • Consent (where required for certain cookies/marketing)

4) How we share information

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the business (such as exam hosting, payments, email delivery, analytics, and website hosting).

We do not sell personal data.

5) Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed to:

  • Provide services and support
  • Maintain credential verification records
  • Meet legal, accounting, or compliance requirements

You may request deletion of your data, subject to necessary retention for legal or credential verification purposes.

6) Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7) Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

To make a request, email support@aiskillsboard.com.

8) Cookies

We may use cookies to improve site performance and understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9) International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than where you live depending on the tools we use. We take reasonable steps to protect your data during such processing.
