  • A) Use one detailed prompt for all 50 prospects.
  • B) Let the AI generate unique messages with minimal guidance.
  • C) Create a template with variables (company, role, pain points) and review sample outputs before sending.

A colleague wants to upload a customer list to a public AI tool to generate personalized marketing copy. What's the best response?
  • A) It's fine if they delete the chat afterward.
  • B) It's fine if the AI tool has a privacy policy.
  • C) They should get verbal approval from their manager first.
  • D) They should anonymize or remove identifying information before uploading.

You're using AI to draft an NDA. Which statement is most accurate?

  • B) AI-generated legal documents are safe if reviewed by a non-lawyer stakeholder.
  • C) AI is trained on legal documents so the output is likely enforceable.
  • D) Using AI for legal documents is always prohibited.
